Part 6:
Storage Installation

Every case should have dedicated storage bays that allow for your storage

We will go through how to install SSD's and HDD's

Depending on the case and how drive bays are configured, SSD and HDD drives may be able to be installed at the same points or using dedicated brackets.

HDD

SSD

Cables

  • Remove the HDD bracket from one of the bays.

  • Slide the mechanical drive into the bracket.

  • Secure the drive to said bracket using screws or screwless mechanism.

  • Slide the bracket and drive back into the bay.

  • Take the SSD bracket and attach the drive to it, lining up the screw holes.

  • Attach the bracket and secure it to the case.

  • Screw in the drive using appropriate screws.

  • Hard drives require two connections as well, one for power and one for data.

  • Both are L-shaped, so plug the SATA (data) and power cable into the right slots in the motherbaord and storage device.

  • Always check your motherboards manual to find out exactly where the proper slots are located.
