Part 7:
Power Supply Installation

The power supply will supply all the juice required for all of your components

Installation will differ depending on which case you have

It's is mandatory that you have acquired a PSU that has the minimum watts required for your system.

Part 2

  • The slot is located usually by searching for a square openening in your case, usually in the bottom, or top corner.

  • Pay attention to what kind of PSU you have. It will make cable management easier, so you dont use unnecessary cables.

  • Fully modular: No cables are permanently connected to the PSU.

  • Semi-modular: Some cables are permanently fixed, usually ATX and CPU power.

  • Non-modular: Every cable is connected and cannot be removed from the PSU.

  • Slide the PSU into the mounting area with the fan facing up or down.

  • Screw in bundled screws (should come with the case) in the four holes at the rear of the case.

  • Ensure the unit is switched off using the rear switch.
