Part 1:
What do you need?

Everyones needs are different. Your PC needs to match your lifestyle and work.

Build your PC to what YOU need.

No matter what type of power you're putting into your build, every PC needs these required parts.

CPU

Brain of the computer.

Intel or AMD
Motherboard

Connects everything together.

Must be compatible with CPU and case size.
RAM

Computer Memory.

Must be compatible with motherboard.
Storage

Where all your content is stored.

SSD or Hard Drive.
GPU

Process images,videos and games.

Check for card length.
Case

Where all the parts go into.

ATX or mini-ATX.
Power Supply

Gives power to the computer.

Check for minimum power for your system.
Fans

Cools the PC.

Configure inside the case for proper cooling.
